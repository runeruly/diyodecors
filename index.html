The Diyo Essence

Lighting up your celebrations

At DiyoDecors, we draw our inspiration from the traditional 'Diyo' — a sacred oil lamp that symbolizes purity, goodness, and the dispelling of darkness. Just as a Diyo brings a warm, golden glow to its surroundings, we aim to illuminate your special events with breathtaking designs and meticulous attention to detail.

With years of experience in creating luxurious, memorable spaces, founders Umana and Deepa specialize in transforming ordinary venues into magical settings. Whether it's the grandeur of a wedding or the intimate joy of a baby shower, we craft atmospheres that leave lasting impressions. We proudly serve the Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia area.
